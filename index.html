About me

Hi, I’m Priya – a Senior Software Engineer (Full Stack Developer) based in Brisbane, Australia. I have over 8 years of experience in software development, and for the past 5 years, I've been working at a Computer Vision AI company. I began my career as a frontend developer and gradually expanded into backend development, DevOps, and AWS Cloud – now working end-to-end as a full stack developer.

I enjoy building scalable, secure web applications and have hands-on experience with a wide range of technologies, including React.js, Python, Node.js, and AWS services such as Lambda, API Gateway, DynamoDB, S3, CloudFront, SNS, SES, SQS, EC2, ECS, ECR, Quicksight, Athena, Glue, AWS Batch, Cloudwatch, ACM, Cognito, IAM and CloudFormation. I'm also proficient with Docker, CI/CD pipelines, and infrastructure automation. I hold the AWS Certified Solutions Architect - Associate certification.

Outside of work, I’m passionate about education. I’ve volunteered as a tutor for school children in Maths and Tamil and have also taught IT concepts in NSW schools. In my free time, I enjoy gardening, listening to music, and spending time with flowers and nature—it helps me stay balanced and inspired. I love solving problems, learning new things, and contributing to meaningful projects in a collaborative environment.
